1. Executive Summary

ON24 Nexus is the everything app for marketers who care about engagement and data. Every webinar a team runs feeds the system — transcripts, poll results, audience signals, and AI-generated content — making it progressively smarter over time. Nexus lets marketers ask natural-language questions about their event data, create new AI-generated content tuned to their brand voice, explore all existing AI-ACE content, and get data-driven proposals for future webinar schedules and topics — all from a single conversational interface backed by live ON24 data.

The system eliminates the need to navigate 15+ fragmented analytics screens. It also closes the content creation loop: the same AI that generates Key Takeaways from a transcript can write the follow-up blog post, social copy, and eBook in the same brand voice, informed by every piece of content the team has ever produced.

2. Market Problem

Pain Points

  • Analytics fragmentation: ON24 analytics are spread across Dashboard, Smart Tips, Webcast Elite, Engagement Hub, Power Leads, Polls & Surveys, and more — each requiring separate navigation.
  • Time-to-insight: Generating a cross-event attendance trend requires manual export and Excel work — 30–60 minutes for a single answer.
  • Content not reused: AI-ACE generates Key Takeaways, blog posts, and emails after every webinar, but they're buried in the platform and rarely repurposed.
  • Brand inconsistency: When content is repurposed manually, it doesn't match the team's established tone and style.
  • No forward planning intelligence: Teams plan future webinar topics without data on what has worked, which topics are overrepresented, or what audiences want next.
  • Platform how-to barrier: New and occasional ON24 users frequently need to look up how to configure polls, registration pages, or integrations — disrupting their workflow.

Market Opportunity

ON24 serves thousands of enterprise B2B marketing teams. Each team runs 10–200+ webinars annually and accumulates transcripts, poll data, and AI-generated content that is chronically underutilized. ON24 Nexus activates this asset: every webinar makes the system more useful, every piece of content reinforces the brand voice model, and every insight shapes the next event. The compounding value increases with usage — teams that engage consistently gain a feedback loop their competitors don't have.

5. Success Metrics

  • Time-to-first-insight: user gets a post-event performance summary in <30 seconds
  • Platform question deflection: >80% of common how-to questions answered without external links
  • Content creation: users can generate a brand-voice-tuned article from a past webinar in <60 seconds
  • Content discoverability: all AI-ACE articles surfaced and browseable in chat
  • Adoption: >3 queries per active user per week; content creation used by >30% of active users
  • Data accuracy: query results match ON24 platform reports within rounding margin

6. Constraints & Assumptions

  • Data access is read-only against the ON24 master database (no ETL sync needed for reads)
  • Tenant isolation is mandatory — all queries scope to authorized client hierarchy only
  • ON24 write operations (create event, manage registrations) require explicit user confirmation
  • Knowledge base is sourced from ON24 Zendesk help articles (637 articles / 2,729 chunks)
  • Brand voice is derived from existing AI-ACE content in video_library; refreshed monthly via web scrape
  • Transcript-based content creation depends on AI-ACE having processed each event (AUTOGEN_ types present)
  • Requires VPN or ON24 internal network access for the ON24 master database connection
